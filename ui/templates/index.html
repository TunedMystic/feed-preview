About Feed Preview

This microsite is a small utility that helps to preview social embeds within RSS Feeds.

Simply provide the URL of the desired social media content, and our microsite handles the embed retrieval and the RSS feed generation.

What is a social embed in an XML feed?

A social embed in an XML feed refers to the integration of social media content, such as posts, images, or videos, directly into an XML feed.

This allows you to preview and display social media content seamlessly within your feed.

What Social embeds are supported?

We support Instagram urls, and use Facebook's Graph API for embed retrieval (instagram oembed_read permissions).

We support Twitter urls, and use the Twitter API for embed retrieval.

We support YouTube urls, and use the YouTube API for embed retrieval.

Privacy Policy

This microsite is dedicated to providing you with RSS feed previews without compromising your privacy.

We want you to know that we neither collect nor store any of your personal information. Rest assured that your data remains entirely secure.

Additionally, our usage of the 'embed_read' permissions from the Instagram API is solely to enhance your experience with RSS feed previews.

Your trust matters to us, and we are committed to maintaining the utmost privacy standards.
